About Me

Hi, I'm Gading, a passionate front-end developer and university student based in Indonesia. I love building clean, responsive websites that are both functional and visually appealing.

I work primarily with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like Tailwind CSS and Bootstrap. I also explore game development using Godot, GameMaker Studio and Unity.

I am always eager to learn new technologies and improve my skills. I enjoy collaborating with others and contributing to open-source projects.

In my free time, I love playing video games, reading books, and exploring new places.
